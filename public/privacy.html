Data Transparency

ThinkPulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026. This page explains what information is processed, how it is used, and the controls available to end users.

Live privacy endpoint Path: /privacy

1. Data We Process

ThinkPulse processes only the information required for extension operations, billing verification, provider routing, and support diagnostics.

2. How Data Is Used

  • To authenticate account sessions and role-based features.
  • To process payment confirmation and access entitlement.
  • To deliver model responses and OCR workflows.

3. Retention and Security

Data retention is limited to operational and compliance needs. Security controls are continuously updated, but no online platform can claim absolute zero risk.

4. User Rights

Users may request correction or deletion of profile-linked records based on applicable legal requirements and service constraints.

ThinkPulse legal experience is unified across all public pages.
