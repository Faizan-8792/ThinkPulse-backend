ThinkPulse Refund Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026. This policy outlines refund review windows, eligibility checks, and payment evidence requirements.

Live refund endpoint Path: /refund-policy

1. Eligible Refund Window

Requests should be submitted as soon as possible after payment. Late requests may be declined when service usage has already consumed paid resources.

2. Required Information

  • Payment reference or transaction identifier.
  • Account email used for purchase.
  • Clear reason for requesting reversal.

3. Review Process

Each request is reviewed case-by-case. Fraud indicators, duplicate claims, or heavy completed usage may result in rejection.

4. Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are initiated from our side quickly, but final settlement timing is controlled by your payment provider or bank.
